# Learning to Reinforcement Learn

**Wang, Kurth-Nelson, Tirumala, Soyer, Leibo, Munos, Blundell, Kumaran & Botvinick** — arXiv (2016).
https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.05763

**Why it's in the course:** the origin of the meta-RL idea — a recurrent network trained across
a distribution of tasks learns a *learning algorithm* in its activations, adapting to a new task
with no weight changes. The ML statement of "learn the learner" (claim (b)); the precursor to the
prefrontal-cortex-as-meta-RL account.

Lead group: **Matthew Botvinick** (DeepMind, Director of Neuroscience Research; Hon. Prof., Gatsby/UCL).
